Job detailsJob TypeFull-timeFull Job DescriptionPosition Summary:
We are looking for a Chief Data Scientist to design, formalize, and implement a data science practice. You will be responsible for growing a team of data scientists and overseeing their activities to ensure proper execution of duties and alignment with the company's overall vision. You will be responsible for the creation of new data science capabilities for the company by envisioning and executing strategies that will influence improvement of business performance by enabling informed decision making. You will lead the development of big data capabilities and utilization as well as coordinate cross-functional analytic initiatives.
The Chief Data Scientist role requires someone who is hands-on and who can model complex problems, discovering insights, and identifying opportunities through the use of statistical, algorithmic, mining, and visualization techniques. You are someone who can strike the right balance between statistical solutions and big data realities to enhance our platform.
In addition to advanced analytic skills, you are also proficient at integrating and preparing large, varied datasets, architecting specialized database and computing environments, and communicating results. You have experience in a variety of methodologies ranging from statistical analysis to machine learning.
This role is based in Atlanta, Georgia.
Essential Functions and Duties:
Proactively work with senior business executives and various departmental heads across the company in order to provide advanced analytic data modeling systems as well as support the creation and evolution of new products and services
Play a leading role in the development and design of the company's data science vision, capabilities, infrastructure, and quarterly/annual roadmap
Grow the company's data science talent through role definition, recruitment, and development of a team of data scientists that will jointly move the company's agenda forward
Design and launch innovative prototypes and complex analytic models, utilizing a blend of contemporary and traditional data mining techniques, applied to both structured and unstructured data sets
Solve real-world business challenges by application of machine learning, artificial intelligence, statistics, and reliability techniques
Guide data collection efforts for data quality standards and recommend new data streams to be captured
Oversee all data-related activities ensuring alignment with departmental and company-wide vision and strategies.
Drive education and evangelization of data sciences throughout the company by communicating the vision and use cases of advanced analytics
Continuously improve the company's data analysis model, creating industry-leading performance through the leveraging of new and creative data-sources, and employing the latest in machine learning technologies and techniques
Visualize and communicate data insights to cross-functional business leaders
Perform other duties as assignedSupervisory Responsibilities:
This position has direct supervisory responsibilities over the Data Science team located in the Atlanta office, as well as any remote employees and including any 3rd party outsourced resources.
