Job detailsJob TypeFull-timeFull Job DescriptionThe data scientist/engineer in the Performance Optimization Branch will perform digital modeling, simulation, analysis, performance optimization, and machine learning tasks to support electronic warfare research and development. Electronic warfare applications include aircraft self-protection, missile warning, hostile fire indication, laser warning, countermeasures, scene generation, atmospheric phenomena, and electro-optical/infrared (EO/IR) signature modeling. The data scientist/engineer will be on an agile team that performs software optimization, verification, validation, and system simulation using dedicated high-performance computing clusters to support sponsor missions.

Job Duties:
Develop innovative algorithms and apply machine learning/deep learning methods to EW applications such as object detection, object recognition, and time series classification
Research novel methods in the field of automated machine learning and algorithm design to solve problems of today and challenges of tomorrow
Create large robust curated datasets from limited real-world supervised examples through simulation, augmentation techniques, and more modern approaches such as GANs
Deliver full trade space of algorithmic solutions within sponsor performance specifications using multiple-objective optimization and analysis techniques
Write and perform software tests supporting software verification and document outcomes
Lead technical tasks, projects, and/or technical teams
Build and deliver technical presentations to stakeholders/sponsors
Perform other duties as assigned
