POSITION SUMMARY
The Senior Data Analyst will drive transforming data across multiple systems into actionable business insights. The incumbent will be responsible for the full lifecycle of analysis including requirements, data acquisition, design, data quality, analysis and reporting. The incumbent will collaborate with multiple teams to develop dashboards, visualizations, metrics, performance indicators and data quality audits; using the data to tell a meaningful story and determine the key to success. The incumbent will foster an analytics culture within Atlanta Public Schools through advocating data-based insights, bolstering data quality oversight and sharing best practices.

ESSENTIAL DUTIES
Acquires data from primary or secondary data sources
Develops and implements databases/data collection systems that optimize statistical efficiency and quality
Identifies data quality issues, reports findings to data owners and facilitates the development of action plans
Interprets data and analyzes results using statistical techniques
Identifies, analyzes, and interprets trends or patterns in complex data sets
Develops dashboards and/or reports to meet the business reporting need to tell a meaningful story
Locates and defines new process improvement opportunities
Defines, designs and manages master data within the reporting systems, including creation, updates, users, user roles
Designs and manages the reporting environment, including data sources, security, and metadata
Troubleshoots the reporting database environment reporting to supervisor when issues arise
Provides technical expertise on data management
Performs other duties as assigned by appropriate administrator
Required to attend scheduled shift during regular business hours as mandated
